How to Submit Your Timesheet

1
Open the link above on your phone, tablet, or laptop. No app to install.
2
Enter your email address and tap Start Timesheet. Your email must match the one Paul has on file — check with him if it doesn't work.
3
Tap a day to expand it, then enter your start time and end time. Hours, overtime, and breaks are calculated automatically.
4
Save Draft any time you like. Your hours are saved on your device. Come back later, enter your email again, and everything is restored right where you left off.
5
At the end of the week, tap Review & Submit. Check the summary, then Confirm & Submit. You'll receive a confirmation email.

Pay Cycle

You are paid fortnightly. Each pay period covers two weeks, running Friday to Thursday.

Example: The pay period 14 March – 27 March 2026 covers two weeks:
• Week 1: Friday 14 Mar – Thursday 20 Mar
• Week 2: Friday 21 Mar – Thursday 27 Mar

Submit each week's hours by end of day Thursday (or as soon as practical after your last shift for the week).
DayRole in the cycle
FridayFirst day of the pay week
ThursdayLast day — submit your timesheet

High risk hoursEnter separately if you worked masked up

Saving Drafts

You don't have to fill in the whole week at once. Enter your hours each day and they save automatically. You can also tap the Save Draft button to see a summary of what you've entered so far.

Drafts are saved on your device only. If you start on your phone, you'll need to finish on the same phone. Drafts won't appear on a different device.

When you come back to the app, enter your email and tap Start Timesheet — your saved hours will load automatically.

Corrections

Made a mistake on a previous week? Use the Correction section at the bottom of the form. Select the week ending date, pick the day, and enter the corrected start and end times. The correction will override that day's hours in Paul's records.

Upcoming Days Unavailable

If you know you'll be away during the next pay period, enter it in the "Upcoming days unavailable" field before you submit. Paul will see this once your timesheet is submitted.

Confirmation Email

Every time you submit, you'll receive an email from Perfection Services Timesheets confirming your hours. Keep this for your records. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or let Paul know.
